About Me

Background

Want to know a little bit about me?

My name is Abdelrahman Khater. I've followed the classic "gifted kid" arc. I grew up in a working-class family in Cairo, Egypt, was top of my class for years, and eventually made it into a top-tier, ultra-competitive high school (STEM Schools), which led to an even more intense, prestige-packed college experience at NYU. Along the way, I hit plenty of walls and started to realize that traits like perfectionism and chasing external validation — things I thought defined me — were actually just early programming. I've been untangling that ever since.
